About Us

Code for the young is an empowerment programme designed by GiREF to teach teenagers on how to make computer applications, mobile applications, animations and web development using computer programming languages. Technology has been found to be the major driver of economic development by improving efficiency in production and service delivery, this has been seen as a hindrance to development in Africa as a result of poor attitude towards building such technology in Africa. One of the aims of GiREF is to equip teenagers with the knowledge of technology to solve some of the socio-economic problems facing Africa. We have a target of training three thousand teenagers within the space of five years, making them first class African programmers.

Our Vision

To produce teenagers that will flood Africa with technological innovation

Our Mission

It is a volunteer programme that seeks to fill the technological gap in Africa by training teenagers how to make computer software.

Our Philosopy

Our goal is to bring to reality the ideas they have been nurturing and give expression to those ideas through programming. We emphasize problem-solving skills; creativity and innovation; team-work and collaboration.
